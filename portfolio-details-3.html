FX Sprinter

Project information

  • Category: App
  • Client: FX Sprinter Forecaster
  • Project date: March, 2024
  • Project URL: Colab

FX Sprinter Forecaster exemplifies the integration of Python, AI, and machine learning. Built on the K Nearest Neighbor algorithm, this financial analysis tool delivers insightful predictions for EUR/USD prices. With a notable training accuracy of 98.02% and a test accuracy of 29.70%, it stands out as a dependable predictor in the ever-changing forex market.
